As promised in the last post, I have added comments! Even though I am talking to the void right now, I believe this will be great for future interactions.

<!--more-->

I wanted a simple solution for running a comment section. That's why I have decided to use Cusdis — it is open-source, easy to set up, simple to manage, with enough room on the free tier to run the cloud version on a static website.

Since I do not think there is a need to share the setup steps in the AI era, I am sharing only my experiences on this blog. No detailed step-by-step guide will be shared here as well. Just ask your Claude to set it up for you, or check the public [repo](https://github.com/MrSydar/mrsydar.github.io) with this website to check my setup.

Cheers 👋
