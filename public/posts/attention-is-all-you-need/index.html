Attention Is All You Need

First post — defining the blog's direction and principles
June 18, 2026 · 2 min · 374 words · Me

Photo of the Moon I took on June 18th. Edited by Gemini.

Every project has a purpose. The reason it exists. The goal it perceives. This blog is not an exclusion and at the very beginning of the sprint I am about to start I want to define its direction right here.

It is not a part of a big plan to become a famous influencer, sell my soul to the sponsors and quit with tons of money (it sounds like an interesting experience, btw). I would say this is the way to develop my social skills, confidence, and hate tolerance. So here is my first public promise — I will add a comments section to this blog to get feedback.

All of this requires social interaction. To get it, I need to attract some attention, and attention appears out of a desire to learn. It happens I have the curiosity for experimenting and building various technical and nerdy stuff which I wish to share, so the attention-knowledge exchange gap between me, the author, and you, the reader, is closed there.

On the 30th of November 2022 the world was irreversibly changed. Now the machines are capable of implementing your thoughts. They get consistently better at generating considerable thoughts as well. The loop is closing, so does the human necessity to apply a cognitive effort. I have also become a part of this process, and to keep my thinking muscles in shape here comes the core principle I will stick with on this blog — none of the natural-language content under this domain shall be generated by a large language model, or any other text-generation method.

I am in the process of opening various social channels: Telegram for the Slavic audience, X for English-speaking interactions, and Instagram (I would have to learn how to use it first) for sharing general life stories. You may want to check them out as well if you wish to have a chat with me, or it happens you are interested about living in Spain. All the links are on the root page.

Peace ✌️

P.S. The post is titled after the famous science paper that has changed the world forever 5 years after it was published in 2017.
